About Me

Hi! I'm Ziming, a 14-year-old high school student with a passion for teaching beginners how to code. I believe learning programming should be fun and relatable — and who better to teach you than someone close to your age?

We’ll work on real projects in Scratch and Python, starting from zero. By the end, you’ll be confident writing your own simple games and scripts!
